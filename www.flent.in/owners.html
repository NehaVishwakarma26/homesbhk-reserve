FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions
How does Flent determine the rent for my property?

We use our algorithm that analyses market trends, your property’s features, and location to offer a competitive, fixed rental income.

What does the rental guarantee mean?

Our rental guarantee ensures that you start receiving rent from Day 21, even if we haven’t placed a tenant by then.

What happens if my property remains vacant?

We work tirelessly to ensure quick placement. If it remains vacant after 20 days, your rental income begins anyway, thanks to our rental guarantee.

Who handles property maintenance?

Flent covers all routine fixtures and repairs, so you don't have to worry about any maintenance.

How are tenants selected?

We do Aadhaar KYC of all of our tenants, perform thorough background checks to ensure that only responsible, high-quality individuals or families rent your property.

Can I visit my property during the lease?

Absolutely! We arrange periodic inspections and can schedule visits at your convenience.

Do I have to invest in furnishing my property?

Nope. We handle the furnishing entirely at our expense, and it’s all designed to maximise your rental income.

What if I want to terminate the agreement before the stipulated end date?

You can terminate the agreement by giving us a 2-month notice, no penalties involved.

What happens if the tenant leaves my home?

If a tenant leaves, we find a replacement quickly so you never experience a gap in rent. You keep receiving rent as scheduled.

How is the agreement structured?

You’ll sign a principal-agent agreement with Flent, giving us the rights to manage your property, find and vet tenants, and furnish the home. Once a tenant is found, a tripartite agreement between you, Flent, and the tenant is signed.

What if I want to take my home back?

At the end of the lease, you can decide whether to renew or take your property back. If you choose the latter, we’ll return it in the exact condition it was handed over.
